In the GEO Datasets Search bar, perform an advanced boolean query such as:

```text
"Homo sapiens"[Organism] AND "expression profiling by high throughput sequencing"[Strategy] AND ("neuron" OR "neuronal" OR "iPSC") AND ("IFN" OR "interferon" OR "IL-2" OR "TNF" OR "inflammation" OR "cytokine") AND 6:500[Number of Samples]
```
